Here are two examples of identifying the repetend and period of a repeating decimal:

  1. In the repeating decimal 0.\(\overline{3}\text{,}\) the repetend is 3 and the period is 1.

  2. In the repeating decimal 0.00\(\overline{142857}\text{,}\) the repetend starts in the thousandths place and is 142857, and the period is 6.
