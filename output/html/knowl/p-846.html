Now let's subtract the rightmost place. Note that the top 2 is smaller than the bottom digit, so we must borrow. The the place directly to the left has a non-zero top digit, so we regroup 1 of this place (tens) to make 10 of our current place (ones) and thus decrease the digit in that place by 1. Now we have \(2+10=12\) ones and we do the subtraction \(12-7=5\text{.}\)
