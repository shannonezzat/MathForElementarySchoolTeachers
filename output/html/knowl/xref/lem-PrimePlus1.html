Lemma 6.3.4.

Let \(a_1,a_2 \ldots a_n \in \mathbb{N}\) be \(n\) numbers, and let \(a_* = a_1 \times a_2 \times \ldots \times a_n\text{.}\) Then for each \(a_i\) from \(a_1\) to \(a_n\) we have that \(a_i \nmid (a_*+1)\text{.}\)
