Let's multiply \(348 \times 97\text{.}\) First, we write the numbers lined up by place value, but we are thinking of these numbers in expanded form: \((300 + 40 + 8) \times (90 + 7). \) Pictorally, we can view this as breaking up a rectangle with side lengths \(348\) and \(97\) units into sub rectangles where the side lengths are the terms in each expanded form.
