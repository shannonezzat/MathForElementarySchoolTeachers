Example 2.6.7.

In \(\mathbb{N}\text{,}\) addition and multiplication are associative. For example, \((4 \times 3) \times 2 = 4 \times (3 \times 2)\) and \((3 + 7)=4 = 3+ (7 +4)\text{.}\) We can view these pictorally:
