To convert a repeating decimal to a fraction, we can use the following algorithm:

  1. Let x be the number in decimal form.

  2. Let p be the period of the repetend.

  3. Calculate (10^p)x - x in repeated decimal form.

  4. Solve for x.

  5. Convert any numerators and denominators to integers by multiplying both by the appropriate power of 10 to clear decimal places.

  6. Simplify by converting the fraction to lowest terms.
