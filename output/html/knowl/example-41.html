Example 2.6.7.

In \(\mathbb{N}\text{,}\) addition and multiplication are associative. For example, \((3 \times 2) \times 4 = 3 \times (2 \times 4)\) and \((3 + 7)=4 = 3+ (7 +4)\text{.}\) We can view these pictorally:

Now in \(\mathbb{N},\) subtraction is *not* associative. As a counterexample, we have \((6-4)-1 = 2-1 =1\) but \(6-(4-1) = 6-3 = 3\text{.}\)
