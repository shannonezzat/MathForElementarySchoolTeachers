Consider the division problem \(a \div b\) where \(a,b \in mathbb{N}.\) The idea behind long division uses the partitive mode of division along with the idea of place value. Starting with the largest place value of the dividend \(a\text{,}\) we group as many of those place into \(b\) groups. Any of that place value remaining we ungroup into the next largest place value.
