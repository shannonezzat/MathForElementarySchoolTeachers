Item 2.

Even really just means "divisible by 2". We can (and will in subsequent chapters) extend this idea to express a number \(n\) being divisible by some other number \(c\) meaning \(n=c \times k\) for some whole number \(k\text{.}\) The idea of "odd" extends similarly, though you can have more than one part remaining. This is basically the idea of remainders when doing division.
